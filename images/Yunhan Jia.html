SHORT BIO

My name is Yunhan (Jack) Jia. I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering @ University of Michigan, working with Professor Z. Morley Mao.

I received my B.E. in Software Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2013, advised by Professor Haibo Chen.

My research interest is in networking and security.

Research Stetement

I am working on investing the security and safety requirement for appified platform (including smartphone, IoT, connected vehicle), and improving the access control mechanism in different context. My past research includes:

  • Unveiling the security implication of open ports on mobile platform.
  • Accelerated diagnosis for cellular network services.
